What

Shield protect user from DDoS attack

Tiers

Standard

  1. Enabled by default for every user
  2. Protect such as SYN/UDP floods, reflection attacks and other L3/L4 attacks

Advanced

  1. Optional DDoS mitigation service
  2. $3000 per month per org
  3. Protect against more sophisticated attacks such as EC2, ELB, CloudFront, Global Accelerator, Route 53.
  4. 24/7 access to AWS DDoS Response Team
  5. Create WAF ACL rules on WAF for your to protect against DDoS attacks.
